## Overview

Fiverr's algorithm is **multi-factor, performance-weighted, and constantly evolving**. Only **12% of Fiverr gigs ever reach page 1**. This skill teaches you how to be in that 12%.

**Key insight:** The 2026 algorithm heavily weighs seller performance + buyer satisfaction over keyword density alone. Speed, reviews, and completion rate = ranking success.

---

## THE ALGORITHM: 4 Pillars (Weighted Factors)

### 1. KEYWORDS & RELEVANCE (25-30% of algorithm)
How well does your gig match what buyers are searching for?

**Factors:**
- Gig title (single most important ranking factor)
- Gig tags (5-7 tags maximum)
- Gig category + subcategory
- Keyword placement and density (natural, not stuffed)
- Description keyword relevance

**Optimization:**
- Title = buyer search intent + your differentiator
- Example: Instead of "I will do data entry" → "Fast Data Entry: 100+ Rows in 5 Minutes | Excel Cleanup"
- Tags = buyer-facing keywords (what they type into search)
- Natural keyword usage (avoid repetition = algorithm penalty)

### 2. SELLER PERFORMANCE (30-35% of algorithm)
Your track record as a seller matters MORE than keywords in 2026.

**Factors:**
- Order completion rate (target: 100% — never cancel your own orders)
- On-time delivery rate (target: 95-100%, build buffer time)
- Average response time to messages (target: <2 hours)
- Gig completion velocity (how many jobs you complete per week)
- Repeat customer rate (repeat = better algorithm signal)

**Optimization:**
- Always deliver 24-48 hours BEFORE deadline (buffer time)
- Respond to all buyer messages within 1 hour (set alerts)
- Never cancel orders yourself (unless emergency)
- Build delivery momentum (complete 5+ orders before expecting page 1)

### 3. BUYER SATISFACTION (25-30% of algorithm)
Reviews + ratings + buyer behavior signals

**Factors:**
- Star rating (4.8+ = page 1 candidate)
- Review count (more reviews = trust signal)
- Review recency (recent 5-star = boost)
- Revision rate (high revisions = algorithm penalty)
- Buyer report rate (complaints = penalty)
- Repeat purchase rate from same buyer

**Optimization:**
- Quality > quantity (1 perfect job = better than 5 mediocre)
- Revision prevention = critical (clear scope = fewer revisions)
- Encourage reviews (ask satisfied buyers: "Would you recommend?")
- Avoid refunds (= algorithm red flag)

### 4. ENGAGEMENT & CONVERSION (10-15% of algorithm)
How do buyers interact with your gig?

**Factors:**
- Click-through rate (CTR) from search results
- View-to-order conversion rate
- Time spent on gig page (buyers reading your description)
- Gig video presence (videos = 20-30% CTR boost)
- Gig image quality (professional thumbnail = CTR boost)

**Optimization:**
- Professional gig image/thumbnail (first 3 seconds matter)
- Gig video (30-60 sec, show your value prop)
- Clear, scannable description (buyers skim, don't read)
- Strong CTA in description ("Ready to start immediately")
- Highlight speed/quality/uniqueness in title

## KEYWORD RESEARCH STRATEGY

### Step 1: Find Buyer Intent Keywords
**NOT:** High-volume keywords (too competitive)  
**YES:** Buyer-intent keywords (people ready to buy)

**How:**
1. Go to Fiverr search bar
2. Start typing your service (e.g., "data entry")
3. **Note autocomplete suggestions** — these = high-demand keywords
4. Example:
   - "data entry"
   - "data entry fast"
   - "data entry excel"
   - "data entry copy paste"
   - "data entry 24 hours"

**Why this works:** Fiverr's autocomplete = actual buyer searches

### Step 2: Analyze Competitor Gigs
1. Search top 3 keywords
2. Look at page 1 gigs (top 10)
3. **Note their titles:** What words do they use?
4. **Note their tags:** Check page source (Ctrl+U) for tag keywords
5. **Note their pricing:** What's the market rate?
6. **Note their reviews:** What do buyers praise?

### Step 3: Identify Keyword Difficulty
| Competition | # of Gigs | New Seller Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Low** | <500 | Start here (easier to rank) |
| **Medium** | 500-2,000 | Move here after 10 reviews |
| **High** | 2,000-5,000 | Avoid initially (target after 50+ reviews) |
| **Very High** | >5,000 | Ignore (established sellers only) |

**New seller strategy:** Target 2-3 LOW competition keywords first, then expand as you level up.

### Step 4: Long-Tail Keyword Advantage
**Long-tail keywords** (3-5 words) = easier ranking + better buyers

Examples:
- ❌ "data entry" (too broad, 10K+ gigs, impossible to rank)
- ❌ "excel help" (still too broad)
- ✅ "fast data entry spreadsheet" (400 gigs, rankable)
- ✅ "copy paste data cleanup 24 hours" (50 gigs, very rankable)
- ✅ "organize excel list 5 minutes" (100 gigs, rankable + specific)

**Rule:** Longer = less competition = easier ranking

## GIG TITLE OPTIMIZATION

### The Formula
```
[Keyword] [Benefit] [Differentiator/Speed]
```

**Examples:**

❌ **Bad:** "I will do data entry"
- Generic, no keyword, no benefit, no speed

✅ **Good:** "Fast Data Entry: 100+ Rows in 5 Minutes | Excel Cleanup"
- Keyword: "data entry"
- Benefit: "Excel cleanup"
- Speed: "5 minutes"
- Tangible: "100+ rows"

❌ **Bad:** "Excel Help"
- Too broad, no specificity

✅ **Good:** "Excel Formulas & VLOOKUP Fixes: 24-Hour Turnaround"
- Keywords: "Excel Formulas," "VLOOKUP"
- Benefit: "Fixes"
- Speed: "24-Hour"

❌ **Bad:** "Writing Services"

✅ **Good:** "Professional Product Descriptions: 50 Words, SEO-Optimized, $5"
- Keywords: "Product Descriptions," "SEO"
- Benefit: "Professional"
- Specificity: "50 Words"
- Price: "$5" (call out value)

### Title Rules
- **Character limit:** 80 characters
- **Keyword placement:** First 3-5 words ideally
- **Avoid:** Keyword stuffing, ALL CAPS (looks spammy), excessive punctuation
- **Include:** Number, speed, or specific outcome when possible
- **Test:** If your title appears in Fiverr autocomplete, it's working

## GIG TAGS STRATEGY

**Tags:** 5-7 keyword phrases buyers search for

### Tag Structure
- **Tag 1:** Main keyword (e.g., "data entry")
- **Tag 2:** Long-tail variant (e.g., "excel data entry")
- **Tag 3:** Benefit-focused (e.g., "fast data entry")
- **Tag 4:** Specific pain point (e.g., "data cleanup")
- **Tag 5:** Speed/outcome (e.g., "24-hour turnaround")
- **Tag 6:** Service type (e.g., "virtual assistant")
- **Tag 7:** Niche modifier (e.g., "accuracy guaranteed")

### Example Tag Set (Data Entry)
1. "data entry" (main)
2. "excel data entry" (specific)
3. "fast data entry" (speed)
4. "spreadsheet cleanup" (problem)
5. "copy paste tasks" (service)
6. "24-hour completion" (speed)
7. "accuracy guaranteed" (benefit)

**Rule:** Mix main keywords + long-tail + benefit-focused

## DESCRIPTION OPTIMIZATION

### Structure
```
[HOOK: One sentence problem/solution]

[WHAT I DO: Bullet list of deliverables]

[WHY ME: Proof/experience/speed/quality]

[EXAMPLES: Specific use cases]

[PERFECT FOR: Ideal buyer profile]

[CTA: Clear next step]
```

### Example Description (Data Entry)
```
Need data moved, organized, or cleaned up FAST? 
I'll handle it in minutes, not hours.

WHAT I DO:
✓ Copy data from one source to another
✓ Organize messy spreadsheets
✓ Format lists and databases

✓ Verify accuracy (99.9%)

WHY ME:

- 100+ successful projects
- Always on-time (24-48 hr delivery)
- Never have revision requests (clear scope)

EXAMPLES:
- PDF → Excel migration
```

### Description Optimization Checklist

## GIG VIDEO IMPACT

**Video effect:** +20-30% CTR boost vs non-video gigs

### Video Best Practices
- **Length:** 30-60 seconds max
- **Content:**
  - Show your workspace
  - Demo of your work (sample project)
  - Testimonial (if available)
  - Your value prop (speed/quality/price)
- **Quality:** Clear audio, good lighting, professional
- **Thumbnail:** Your face or eye-catching visual (first 2 sec)

### Video Script Template
```
[0-5 sec] Hook: "Need [problem solved]? You're in the right place."
[5-15 sec] What you do: Show sample project, explain what you delivered
[15-25 sec] Why hire you: Speed, quality, experience, guarantee
[25-30 sec] CTA: "Ready? Message me. Let's get started."
```

## RED FLAGS & PENALTIES

### Algorithm Penalties (Kills Ranking)
❌ Keyword stuffing — "data entry data entry data entry excel excel excel"
❌ Misleading title — Title says $5, actual price is $50
❌ Fake reviews — Fiverr detects coordination, de-ranks
❌ High cancellation rate — >5% = algorithm penalty
❌ High revision rate — >20% revisions = quality signal failure
❌ Duplicate content — Copy-pasted competitor descriptions
❌ Slow response time — Don't respond for 8+ hours
❌ Late deliveries — Miss deadline even once = ranking hit

### How to Recover
1. **Stop the bleed:** Fix the problem immediately (respond faster, deliver earlier, improve quality)
2. **Quality push:** Deliver 5-10 perfect orders in a row
3. **Wait:** Algorithm takes 2-4 weeks to re-evaluate
4. **Monitor:** Ranking should recover if you've fixed the issue
