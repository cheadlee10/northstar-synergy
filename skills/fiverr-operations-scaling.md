## Platform Mechanics (Know These)

### Fiverr Seller Levels & Limits
| Level | Reviews Needed | Req. Completion % | Order Caps | Visibility | Features |
|-------|---|---|---|---|---|
| **New Seller** | 0 | N/A | ~5-10 concurrent | Low | Standard gigs |
| **Level 1** | 10+ reviews | 90%+ | ~15-20 concurrent | Medium | Priority support, custom offers |
| **Level 2** | 50+ reviews | 95%+ | ~30 concurrent | High | Video profiles, advanced analytics |
| **Top Rated** | 100+ reviews | 98%+ | Unlimited | Highest | Premium badge, surge pricing |

**Strategy:** Ramp through Level 1 (2-3 weeks) ‚Üí Level 2 (6-8 weeks) ‚Üí Top Rated (3 months)

### Delivery Time Requirements
- **Standard:** 5-7 days (default)
- **Express:** 3 days (+50% price multiplier allowed)
- **Rush:** 24 hours (+100% price multiplier)
- **Urgency matters:** 24-48hr deliveries = highest ratings, fastest ranking

### Revenue Model
- **Fiverr takes 20%** of every order (built-in)
- **Payout timing:** 14 days after completion
- **Minimum withdrawal:** $5

**Net calculation:** If John charges $50, he gets $40 after Fiverr's cut.

## Operational Framework

### 1. Gig Setup & Portfolio
**My job:** Help John structure his gig profile for maximum visibility and conversion.

#### Gig Title Optimization
- **Bad:** "I will help with Excel"
- **Good:** "Professional Excel Automation: VBA Macros, Data Cleaning, Dashboards"
- **Rules:** Use keywords, be specific, include 2-3 service types per gig

#### Gig Categories to Start
1. **Data Entry & Cleanup** ($5-15/gig) ‚Äî high volume, low complexity
2. **Excel Formulas & Automation** ($15-35/gig) ‚Äî sweet spot for John
3. **Custom Scripts & Tools** ($35-75/gig) ‚Äî leverage AI for complex work
4. **Business Automation** ($50-100/gig) ‚Äî premium tier, fewer takers but high margin

#### Gig Description Framework
```
HEADLINE (1 line): What problem I solve
WHAT I'LL DELIVER (3-5 bullets): Specific outcomes
WHY ME: Proof points (# clients, years experience, quality focus)
HOW IT WORKS: 3-step process
TIMELINE: Delivery speed
PRICING: Clear per-tier breakdown
```

### 2. Request Filtering & Intake
**My job:** Identify which Fiverr requests John should take (high-margin, low-revision potential).

#### Green Light (Take Immediately)
- ‚úÖ Micro jobs (data entry, copy-paste, small formatting)
- ‚úÖ Repeat clients (revisions less likely, faster)
- ‚úÖ Clear scope (specific deliverable, not fuzzy)
- ‚úÖ 5+ day timeline (no rush premiums needed)
- ‚úÖ Established buyer (good Fiverr rating)

#### Yellow Light (Clarify Before Accepting)
- üü° Medium-scope projects without clear specs
- üü° Buyers with <4 stars or few completed orders
- üü° Rush orders (24-48hr) ‚Äî higher revision risk
- üü° "Custom quote" requests (scope creep potential)

#### Red Light (Skip)
- ‚ùå Projects requiring expertise John doesn't have
- ‚ùå Buyers with < 3.5 stars
- ‚ùå "Ongoing" or "retainer" projects (don't work on Fiverr model)
- ‚ùå "Build my business idea" requests (vague, endless revisions)

### 3. Job Queue Management
**My job:** Optimize the order intake rate and delivery schedule.

#### Ideal Intake Pattern
- **Monday-Wednesday:** Target 15-20 new requests (build pipeline)
- **Thursday-Saturday:** Deliver 10-15 jobs (stagger due dates)
- **Sunday:** Light day (respond to inquiries, update gigs)

#### Delivery Clustering
- **Never:** All jobs due on same day (crunch = quality drops)
- **Instead:** Cluster deliveries across 4-5 days to spread load
- **Example:** Jobs due Mon/Tue/Wed/Thu/Fri (not all Friday)

### 4. Quality Control at Scale
**My job:** Maintain 4.9+ stars while hitting high volume.

#### Pre-Delivery Checklist
- [ ] Output matches gig description exactly
- [ ] No obvious errors (typos, formula bugs, broken links)
- [ ] Formatting is professional (clear, consistent)
- [ ] Documentation included (if applicable)
- [ ] Works on buyer's system (test compatibility)

#### Revision Prevention
- **Upfront clarity:** Ask 2-3 clarifying questions before starting
- **Preview delivery:** For medium/complex jobs, send draft for approval first
- **Templated outputs:** Use standard formats buyers recognize

#### Revision Response (If Necessary)
- **Speed:** Respond within 2 hours (shows care)
- **Attitude:** "Happy to fix that ‚Äî give me X hours"
- **Scope control:** "That's outside our original scope, but happy to quote a revision"

### 5. Pricing & Upselling Strategy
**My job:** Optimize average deal size without losing volume.

#### Tiered Pricing Model
```
Fiverr Gig Level:
- Base: $15 (1 thing)
- +1 extra: $25 (2 things, 1 add-on)
- +2 extras: $35 (3 things, 2 add-ons)
- Premium/Rush: $50-100 (fast turnaround)
```

#### Upsell Tactics
- **During inquiry:** "You could also add custom automation for +$15"
- **After delivery:** "Want me to optimize this further? $25 for X"
- **To website:** "Complex project? Might be better as a custom project ‚Äî ping me for quote"

#### When to Increase Base Price
- **After 10 reviews:** +$5 minimum ($15‚Üí$20)
- **After 50 reviews:** +$10 minimum ($20‚Üí$30)
- **Level 2 status:** +20% across board
- **Top Rated:** +30-50% (market will bear it)
